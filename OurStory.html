From the beginning

In the dawn of an evening that seemed to promise no more than a simple gathering among friends in a modest garden, the spark emerged that would redefine the concept of fun and community. With hearts full of enthusiasm and ignited imagination, we gave birth to a legend named "Backyard".

The beginning was modest, with a low-budget party in the backyard of the house. But in the light of dancing flames and under the starry sky, we felt the warmth of an authentic connection and the energy of a future full of promise. That was the moment when the name that would become synonymous with authentic fun and memorable experiences was born: Backyard. With each new party, we added something, transforming our modest garden into a sanctuary of joy and free expression. A talented DJ and inspired arrangement turned Backyard into an oasis of fun and good vibes. And, as is often the case in such stories, what began as a modest gathering among friends evolved into a major event, gathering 100 souls eager for adventure and unexpected experiences.

But we didn't stop there. With each party, we added new layers of magic and ambition. We conceived Backyard as a place for encounters between free-spirited souls passionate about life, so we decided to add an entrance fee, turning the event into an exclusive and memorable experience for all who graced us with their presence. With each new edition of Backyard, we felt that destiny was urging us for more. So we made a bold decision: to search for a space that truly reflected our essence and ambition. And thus, "The Home of Backyard" was born from our desire to offer our community a place for their own stories.

With the passage of time and with the help of a devoted and vibrant team, Backyard has become more than just a location - it has become a symbol of liberation and authentic joy. With each passing day, we strive to offer unique and memorable experiences, welcoming you with the warmest and most vibrant vibe, and the 10 years of experience have shown us that we are on the right path. So, we invite you to join us on our journey towards happiness and adventure, because Backyard is not just a location or a party - it is a way of life, a liberation from the ordinary, and a journey towards authentic joy and human connection. So let's dance under the stars and create memories that will endure forever in our hearts!
